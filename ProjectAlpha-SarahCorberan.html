Why Design? Why not marketing, law, medicine or anything else?

That is the question they constantly asked her. Well, the answer has always been quite simple, art and design is her passion.

Since an early age, she has always been intuitive regarding colours, shapes, constantly asking questions to herself about the style of design and its meaning to the designer.

Art for her has a powerful meaning, it is more than words could describe. Not only has she grown in and around the industry of design because of her father, but it also has allowed her to express herself and escape from reality in her darker days.

The impact of her father's profession as an Industrial Designer growing up and still to this day, he is her biggest inspiration. Development of her own personal style is important to differentiate herself from the norm. Illustration, product and editorial design are her main focus.

However personally, she would like to take her knowledge of Graphic design further. By studying other types of design, she further expands her knowledge base and unique style.
